I am a biology student currently studying at the University of California, Riverside. My goal is to be a orthopedic doctor of physical therapy, or DPT. My interest in the subject comes from the fact that by nature, I am quite injury prone. I have broken five bones, had two surgeries, and had numerous connective tissue issues all over my body throughout my life. Coupled with my deep love for lifting (which actually has never caused a bone issue), I understand quite a bit about human anatomy and the mechanics behind movements that the human body is capable of. Because of this knowledge and the pain I have experienced in my life, I want a way to help others who are struggling similarly in a way that allows me to connect with them and utilize my social skills for good around me.
